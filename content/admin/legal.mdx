## Key policies

### Charitable solicitation
MDplus is registered to solicit donations in states requiring registration. The Communications Lead maintains registration status.

### Lobbying limitations
As a 501(c)(3), MDplus has strict limits on lobbying activity. Any advocacy work must be reviewed by the Executive Director.

### Unrelated business income
Revenue from activities not related to our exempt purpose may be taxable. Consult with the Treasurer before launching new revenue streams.

<Callout type="tip" title="When in doubt, ask">
If you're unsure whether an activity complies with 501(c)(3) rules, ask the Executive Director or legal counsel before proceeding.
</Callout>
