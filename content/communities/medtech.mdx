## Career pathways

### Medical Director (Device company)
- **Focus:** Clinical input on product development
- **Engagement:** Advisory, part-time, or full-time
- **Value add:** Clinical validation, KOL relationships

### Chief Medical Officer
- **Typical path:** Clinical practice → Medical Director → CMO
- **Responsibilities:** Clinical strategy, regulatory interactions, publications
- **Companies:** Startups to Fortune 500 medtech

### Clinical Research
- **Roles:** PI, Study Chair, Clinical Advisory Board
- **Focus:** Clinical trials for device approval
- **Compensation:** Consulting fees, grants

### Digital Health Product
- **Roles:** Clinical Product Lead, Medical Advisor
- **Focus:** Product-market fit, clinical workflows
- **Companies:** Health apps, telehealth, EHR vendors

## Device regulatory basics

| Pathway | Risk Level | Example | Timeline |
|---------|-----------|---------|----------|
| 510(k) | Low-moderate | BP monitor | 3-6 months |
| De Novo | Novel low-risk | New diagnostic | 6-12 months |
| PMA | High-risk | Implantable | 1-3 years |
