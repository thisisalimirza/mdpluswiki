Central repository for leadership meeting notes and action items. Keep notes structured and action items assigned.

## Meeting note template

Copy this template for each meeting:

```markdown
## [Meeting Name] — [Date]

**Attendees:** [Names]
**Facilitator:** [Name]
**Note-taker:** [Name]

### Agenda
1. Topic one
2. Topic two
3. Open floor

### Discussion notes
[Summary of each agenda item]

### Action items
- [ ] [Task] — [Owner] — Due [Date]
- [ ] [Task] — [Owner] — Due [Date]

### Next meeting
[Date/time] — [Facilitator]
```

## Weekly leadership sync

The weekly leadership sync meets every **Monday at 4pm PT** via Zoom. All active leaders are expected to attend.

### Standing agenda

1. **Check-in** (5 min) — Quick pulse check
2. **Wins & announcements** (5 min) — Celebrate recent successes
3. **Community updates** (15 min) — Round-robin from each community lead
4. **Operations updates** (10 min) — Chief of Staff
5. **Strategic discussion** (20 min) — One deep-dive topic per week
6. **Action item review** (5 min) — Status of outstanding items

<Callout type="info" title="Meeting recordings">
All weekly syncs are recorded and stored in the shared Google Drive under `Leadership > Meeting Recordings`. Recordings are available for 90 days.
</Callout>

## Monthly all-hands

The monthly all-hands includes all MDplus community members. Leadership coordinates the agenda and logistics.

- **When:** First Friday of each month, 12pm PT
- **Where:** Zoom (link posted in #announcements)
- **Duration:** 60 minutes
- **Recording:** Posted to YouTube within 48 hours

## Quarterly planning sessions

Quarterly planning happens in the first two weeks of each quarter. The process:

1. **Week 1:** Community leads submit OKR proposals
2. **Week 2:** Leadership reviews, aligns, and finalizes
3. **Quarter kickoff:** Announce OKRs to full community

<Callout type="tip" title="Archive old notes">
At the end of each quarter, archive meeting notes to Google Drive and start fresh. Keep only the current quarter's notes active in Slack.
</Callout>
