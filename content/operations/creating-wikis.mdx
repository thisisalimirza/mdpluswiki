## Importing from Slack Channels

Over the years we have accumulated a lot of information in our Slack channel. This is institutional knowledge and we want to preserve it. It is also unwieldy to search for information in Slack. We also have lots of verticals and sub-verticals who with a fair amount of autonomy in how they operate. We want to maintain that, while still making sure we have good cross-vertical communication and coordination. 

If External Relations decides they want to start using a Google Form to collect reuqests for partnerships from the Growth and Community team, we want to make sure that the form is accessible to the Growth and Community team without them having to now keep track of XR's messages and updates.

A good starting point is to import your current essential knowledge and build from there.

### Importing from Slack

1. Download and setup Claude Desktop app
2. Go int Claude's CoWork mode.
3. Setup the connector for Slack.

## Building Your Wiki De Novo

1. Click Edit Page or New Page
2. Input your name so we can track changes
3. Type out whatever you want. 
4. Look at the Preview to see how it looks
5. Hit Save & Commit
6. Refresh after a minute or two to see your changes.
