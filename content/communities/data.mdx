The Data community focuses on healthcare data science, clinical informatics, and health AI/ML applications.

## Community overview

<PersonRow name="[Lead Name]" role="Community Lead" email="data@mdplus.community" slack="#data" />

**Mission:** Empower physician-innovators to leverage data science and AI in healthcare settings.

**Focus areas:**
- Clinical data science and analytics
- Healthcare AI/ML applications
- Electronic health record (EHR) data
- Population health analytics
- Clinical decision support systems
