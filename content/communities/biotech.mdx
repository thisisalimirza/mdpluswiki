## Career pathways

### Medical Affairs
- **Roles:** MSL, Medical Director, VP Medical Affairs
- **Focus:** Scientific exchange, KOL engagement
- **Entry:** Often direct from clinical practice

### Clinical Development
- **Roles:** Medical Monitor, Clinical Development Lead
- **Focus:** Trial design, safety monitoring, regulatory submissions
- **Entry:** Pharma fellowship or direct hire

### Regulatory Affairs
- **Roles:** Regulatory Strategist, CMC Lead
- **Focus:** FDA interactions, submission strategy
- **Entry:** Usually requires regulatory experience

### Business Development
- **Roles:** BD&L, Alliance Management
- **Focus:** Licensing, partnerships, M&A
- **Entry:** Often requires MBA or consulting background

<Callout type="tip" title="Start with consulting or advisory">
Many MDs break into biotech through expert network calls or consulting engagements before making a full-time transition.
</Callout>
