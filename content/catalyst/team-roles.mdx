## Workstream descriptions

### Application
Owns the Google Form application — building it, keeping it updated, and fixing issues flagged by applicants. Point of contact for form logic bugs (e.g., ranking, Thalamus listing issue). Ensures the responder link (`https://forms.gle/Z8BUh3mVqZkJsjPK9`) is the one shared in all outreach, not the editor link.

### Student Outreach
Drives proactive outreach to medical schools via email listservs and professional networks. Maintains the [school outreach tracker](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1e7S0XhXCi21lVs4mzvRys_cy5eUbJDXybRLiXUxD3gk/edit) and uses the [outreach templates doc](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1-dSO5xDdcBvtgngkHA9Ch58VWIjQnJirTM9TU6OxKg0/edit). Log every school contacted so the team doesn't duplicate outreach.

### Marketing
Manages all social media promotion across Instagram, LinkedIn, and X. Produces Canva assets (reels, carousels, static posts). Coordinates with outreach team to align messaging. Directs all posts to the fellowship landing page and application form.

### App Review Team
Reviews submitted applications during the mid–late May window to select fellows. Will also handle matchmaking teams to projects. Sherman does a final pass. Reviewers should align on a rubric before the window opens — Maya Roytman offered to help build the rubric.

### Fellowship Engagement
Keeps cohort energy high throughout the 10 weeks: encourages teams to demo, share progress, get peer feedback, and connect with each other. Organizes community touchpoints inside the cohort.

### Mentor Engagement
Ensures mentors stay active throughout the fellowship — at minimum one check-in per week with their assigned team. Troubleshoots mentor no-shows and coordinates escalations. Also responsible for tagging mentors in the sponsor/mentor tracker by interest area so matching is easier.

### Technical Mentors & Ticketing
Handles technical support for fellow teams — debugging, deployment issues, and async unblocking. Sherman is exploring a dedicated help channel for teams to get quick answers. Anthropic will provide 1–2 workshop leaders to onboard teams onto Claude.

### Curriculum
Designs and curates the weekly programming: speaker of the week, office hours scheduling, informing fellows about upcoming sessions. Works with sponsors and mentors to coordinate workshop slots.

### Sponsorship Relations
Manages ongoing relationships with confirmed sponsors — scoping their projects, reviewing proposals, and keeping communication flowing. Owns the [sponsor/mentor tracker](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1TAZc7t-KUwVKozM-sXHS0quvbXNKKK_ZrUYUEy2a1LU/edit). Andrew Parambath leads outreach for new sponsor closes; Rishma Jivan and Emily Leventhal support relationship management and project scoping.
