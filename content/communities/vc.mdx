## Pathways into VC

### Venture Fellow programs
Many VC firms offer part-time "Venture Fellow" or "Venture Partner" roles for practicing clinicians:
- **Commitment:** 5-10 hours/week
- **Compensation:** Typically carry (equity) rather than salary
- **Duration:** 1-2 year terms, often renewable

### Angel investing
- **Minimum check:** Usually $1,000-$25,000
- **Platforms:** AngelList, SyndicateList, alumni networks
- **Time commitment:** Variable, deal-by-deal

### Full-time VC
- **Roles:** Associate, Principal, Partner
- **Background:** Usually requires MBA or significant operating experience
- **Transition:** Most common after fellowship or startup experience

<Callout type="warning" title="Accreditation requirements">
Many investment opportunities require "accredited investor" status. Understand SEC requirements before committing capital.
</Callout>
