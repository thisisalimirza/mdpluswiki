## Career pathways

### Management consulting
- **Target firms:** McKinsey, BCG, Bain, Deloitte, Accenture
- **Typical roles:** Healthcare practice, life sciences strategy
- **Prep timeline:** 6-12 months of case practice

### Expert networks
- **Platforms:** GLG, Guidepoint, AlphaSights
- **Engagement:** Paid consultations ($300-1000/hour)
- **Topics:** Clinical expertise, market insights

### Advisory boards
- **Types:** Pharma advisory, startup advisory, hospital boards
- **Compensation:** Honoraria, equity, or retainer-based

<Callout type="tip" title="Start with expert networks">
Expert network calls are the fastest way to get paid consulting experience. Most MDs can start within 2-4 weeks of signing up.
</Callout>
