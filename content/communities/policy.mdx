## Engagement opportunities

### White House Fellowship
The most prestigious policy fellowship for physicians:
- **Duration:** 1 year
- **Placement:** Executive branch agencies
- **Application:** Opens each fall

### Congressional health staffer
Serve as health policy advisor to a member of Congress:
- **Roles:** Health LA, committee staff
- **Duration:** 1-2 years typical
- **Salary:** $50,000-$80,000

### Agency appointments
Work at HHS, FDA, CMS, NIH, or CDC:
- **Types:** Political appointees vs. career staff
- **Entry points:** Fellowships, details, direct hire

### State-level engagement
- Medical board participation
- State health department roles
- Medicaid advisory committees

<Callout type="info" title="AMA and specialty societies">
Organized medicine (AMA, specialty societies) offers structured policy engagement through councils, committees, and advocacy days.
</Callout>
