## Use cases in healthcare

### Health data ownership
- Patient-controlled health records
- Consent management on-chain
- Data marketplaces for research

### Clinical research (DeSci)
- Decentralized trial coordination
- Tokenized research funding
- Open-access publication incentives

### Supply chain
- Pharmaceutical track-and-trace
- Medical device provenance
- Counterfeit prevention

### Payments and credentialing
- Direct patient-provider payments
- Cross-border health payments
- Physician credential verification

## Getting started

1. **Learn the basics** — Understand blockchain fundamentals (consensus, smart contracts)
2. **Pick a use case** — Focus on a specific healthcare application
3. **Join the community** — Participate in #blockchain discussions
4. **Build something** — The best learning is hands-on

<Callout type="warning" title="Approach with healthy skepticism">
Healthcare blockchain is nascent and overhyped in some areas. Focus on real problems, not technology for its own sake.
</Callout>
